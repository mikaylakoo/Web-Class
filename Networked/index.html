Cheer Culture

My favourite time of the year is when there's an international sports competition - Olympics, World Cup, etc.

Throughout my life living in four distinct countries, everyone was so passionate about their national teams and created a special cheer.

In Korea, we had a world cup cheer, in New Zealand we had a rugby team cheer, in Michigan they had football cheer, in Toronto they had basketball cheer.

Each country that I lived, I became united with the entire nation under the same cheer and made me feel like I was part of everyone's culture.

I want to relive that moment and create the oneness with every nation's sports cheer!
